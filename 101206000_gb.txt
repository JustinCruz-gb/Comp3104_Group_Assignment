George Brown is named after the actual George Brown(Born November 29, 1818, died May 9, 1880)

George Brown(the College) was founded in 1966 and opened in 1967.

In total, from the 3 campuses, there are 35 diploma programs, 31 advanced programs, 8 degree programs
and one in degree in conjunction with Ryerson University
